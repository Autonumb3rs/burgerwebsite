Menu List

Burgers

Regular Burger

A simple yet satisfying combination of tender pork patty and melted cheese nestled between soft buns, delivering a comforting classic taste.

Beef Burger

A hearty feast featuring double beef patties, topped with fresh onions, tomatoes, crisp lettuce, and oozing melted cheese, all sandwiched between fluffy buns for an indulgent bite.

BBQ Burger

Indulge in the smoky flavors of grilled pork and beef patties embraced by toasted buns, crowned with a generous layer of melted cheese, creating a mouthwatering harmony of barbecue goodness.

Deluxe Burger

Elevate your burger experience with a symphony of flavors, boasting crisp lettuce, tangy onions, juicy tomatoes, and a tantalizing blend of special sauce, mustard, and ketchup, all complemented by zesty pickles, nestled within soft buns for a deluxe treat.

Fries

Classic Fries

Crispy golden fries made from regular potatoes, offering a timeless sidekick to your meal, perfect for dipping or enjoying on their own.

Garlic Fries

Elevate your fry game with a savory twist of garlic flavor, enhancing the crispy texture of golden fries for a delightful burst of taste in every bite.

Kamote Fries

Embark on a unique culinary journey with fries crafted from sweet potatoes, offering a subtly sweet and satisfying alternative to traditional fries, perfect for those seeking a wholesome snack.

Deluxe Fries

Experience the ultimate fry sensation with a tantalizing medley of regular, garlic, and sweet potato fries, generously topped with savory ground beef, creating a mouthwatering fusion of flavors and textures.
